    EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE.

    I am currently a computer science student at the University of Maryland, where I have received the prestigious President's Scholarship and am a member of the competitive QUEST Honors Program. My coursework includes a range of topics such as data science, algorithms, programming languages, computer systems, and more.

    I have gained practical experience through a number of internships and entrepreneurial ventures. In addition, I have interned as a frontend web developer at Manifest and continue to support software operations at a Chicago Hindi radio station, Hindi88.fm.

    Through my experience with CraniUS and Manifest, I’ve honed my skills in AWS and Cloud Computing. With CraniUS I’ve created and deployed dockerized full-stack web application with full continuous integration and continuous delivery through AWS ECS and ECR.
